Bioloop

Reduces microplastic pollution. Bioloop fabrics do not biodegrade through following processes. Recyclable. Non-toxic for the environment. Biodegradation tests are made and confirmed by independent testing organizations related to ASTM standards. 

Dope Dyed

Custom-made solution in coloring with eco-friendly method and nearly no water consumption. No charge differences, no color difference between the first meter and the last meter of the yarn. Hundreds of color studies are carried out every year in line with customer demands. 98% less electricity consumption than bobbin dyeing.
